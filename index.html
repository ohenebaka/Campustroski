About Campus Troski

Fast, Affordable and Reliable

Campus troski the brand name of our transportation service is aimed at conveying student who live in apartments and hostels as well as lecturers off campus in its plush 32 sitter bus.

It would start its operation early in the morning, mid- morning, afternoon and in the evening since students would move in and out of campus. Providing students with fast transportation and security, we have an upper hand since we provide snacks and transportation at affordable prices, Our driver Sylvanus has attained a driving license ( which makes him eligible to drive the bus) and Helina is our certified chef.
